CyberCipher

Additive

The Additive Cipher, also known as the Caesar Cipher,it is a cipher that is shifts each letter in the plaintext a certain number of places down or up the alphabet.

Playfair

The Playfair Cipher is a digraphic substitution cipher where each plaintext letter is replaced by a unique pair of ciphertext letters based on a 5x5 grid of letters.

Multiplicative

The Multiplicative Inverse Cipher, a type of affine cipher, encrypts plaintext by a predetermined key, followed by modular arithmetic to ensure decryption is possible.
